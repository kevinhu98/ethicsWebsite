This revolutionary algorithm promised to give students personalized coursework based on their academic performance. By feeding it student data, the algorithm could quickly learn about a student and predict trends and areas of improvement.

As this was a private school, the parents only seemed to care about the results, which it brought.

The teachers on the otherhand, believed that students were more than just a score. After much protesting, the remaining teachers were let go and replaced with personalized teaching AI.
All of this had only occured a few months ago.
